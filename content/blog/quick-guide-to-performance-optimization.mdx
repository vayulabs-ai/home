# Perceived Speed Matters

Users judge speed by how quickly they can interact. Small wins like skeleton screens and prioritized content delivery often outperform big rewrites.

---

## Low-Hanging Wins

- Defer non-critical JavaScript
- Use critical CSS
- Optimize images and use modern formats
- Cache aggressively at the edge

These changes usually yield immediate improvements.

---

## Measure and Repeat

Profiling tools reveal hotspots. Fix the largest issues first and iterate in small, measurable steps.

---

## Closing

Performance is continuous work. Make it part of your PR checklist and ship improvements incrementally.
